HOW TO PLAY

Two players alternate turns on an 11×11 grid. Build an army, claim territory, and destroy every enemy unit to win.

Starting Out

Player 1 starts with $60. Player 2 starts with $70 (slight compensation for going second). On your turn you may place units, move units, and attack — up to your action limit.

Every unit starts with 2 HP. A red dot marks a damaged unit (1 HP).

Combat

Move onto an enemy cell to attack. If you beat that unit type, it's destroyed and you take its cell. Same-type vs. same-type: both take 1 damage and you stay in place — either or both may be destroyed. Tower beats nothing; it relies on friendly units to protect it.

Territory

Each unit projects territory onto surrounding cells — but only when within 2 cells of another friendly unit. Towers always project. Contested cells (claimed by both players) belong to neither. Territory radius: Small 1, Medium 2, Large 3, Tower 2.

Income

Collected at the end of your turn: $10 base + $1 per territory cell + $3 per tower you own.

Placement

Units can only be placed in your own (uncontested) territory. Placement is locked once you've moved or attacked this turn.

Movement

Units move up to 2 cells per turn (horizontal/vertical, no diagonals). A unit standing on a ▲ mountain moves 3. Towers cannot move. Each unit moves at most once per turn.

Actions

You start with 3 actions per turn. Every 17 turns the limit increases by 1. The ring next to the action counter shows progress toward the next increase.

Mountains (▲)

Four mountain tiles are on the board. Units cannot be placed on them, but a unit already standing on a mountain gains +1 movement range.

Undo & Restart

Any action this turn can be undone. Restart Turn reverts everything to the start of your turn.
